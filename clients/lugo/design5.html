A neighbourhood café

Lugo is a small place built around good coffee and the people who come for it.

We opened to make the kind of café we always wanted to find. Friendly, unhurried, and full of regulars who feel like they belong — without the polish that pushes people away or the rustic costume that pretends to be something it isn't.

Every cup is poured from beans roasted by independents we trust. Every bake leaves our kitchen the morning it's served. The menu shifts gently through the seasons.

— Lugo
01 The coffee

Single origins, dialled in daily.

A rotating house espresso plus filters from independent roasters across the UK and Europe.

We taste, weigh, and time every brew — but we keep things friendly. If you're not sure what you're after, ask. We'll find the cup that fits.

02 The bakery

Made fresh, each morning.

Croissants, pastries, cakes, and a short rota of seasonal bakes.

Our kitchen runs from before sunrise. Nothing comes in frozen, nothing sits past the day. What we run out of is what we run out of — come early for first pulls.
