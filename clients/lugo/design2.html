[ 01 ] Hello

We make good coffee for the people who walk past our door.

Lugo is small on purpose. Two of us behind the bar, a kitchen that does one thing each day really well, and regulars who feel like family by the second visit.

We pour a rotating house espresso, two filters, and a list of bakes that changes with whatever's good at the market that morning.
