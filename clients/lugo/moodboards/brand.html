Brand guidelines

Cafe Lugo

A warm, neighborly and modern coffee shop brand for Altrincham: relaxed enough for the morning regular, considered enough for good coffee, bakery and simple local hospitality.

01 Essence

Clear coffee, calm space, familiar faces.

Brand idea

Cafe Lugo should feel like a bright local pause: a clean modern café where coffee, bakery and desserts are presented with care, but never with stiffness.

Personality

Warm, direct, tidy, easygoing and quietly confident. The brand speaks like a good host: helpful, human and unforced.

Audience impression

Customers should read Lugo as a reliable neighborhood café for breakfast coffee, work breaks, casual meetups, lunch plates and a sweet treat after errands.

Avoid becoming

Not a faux-luxury patisserie, not a generic beige café, not an overly edgy coffee brand, and not a busy menu-board identity with too many decorative pieces.

02 Logo usage

Use the mark as a friendly café seal, not as decoration.

The provided Lugo logo combines a rounded café seal, latte-art heart and curved wording. It should remain simple, high-contrast and spacious.

Primary use: white mark on Lugo Sage
Light use: for menus, cards and simple print
Small use: social avatar, sticker, receipt footer

Clear space

Keep at least the height of the “LUGO” letters around the mark on every side. On busy photos, place the logo inside a plain sage or black panel.

Minimum size

Print: 28mm wide minimum. Digital: 96px wide minimum. Below this, use only the central latte-heart symbol if a simplified asset is prepared.

Do not

Do not recolor the logo randomly, add shadows, stretch it, place it over cluttered food photography, rotate it, or rebuild the curved type.

05 Layout & spacing

Let the café breathe.

Margins

Desktop pages should use generous left and right margins: 32–48px minimum, with a content container around 1180px wide.

Spacing

Use 80–100px between major website sections. Use 24px between related cards or menu blocks. Avoid cramped promotional layouts.

Grid

Use a simple 12-column web grid or practical 2/3-column layouts. Align text and images cleanly; do not scatter elements diagonally.

Content width

Keep long text to 620–760px. Menu items can be narrower. Full-width imagery should be reserved for interiors or atmosphere.

Alignment

Default to left alignment. Center alignment is acceptable for the logo, small cards, and event notices, but not for long copy.

Compact blocks

Use compact blocks for opening hours, address, dietary notes, Wi-Fi, takeaway, and short menu highlights.

06 Graphic language

Simple marks from café materials.

Line rules

Use fine horizontal rules to separate menu sections, opening hours and small information. This echoes the menu board and keeps the system tidy.

Rounded seal

The logo’s circular seal can inspire simple round labels: “Fresh today”, “Takeaway”, “New cake”. Use sparingly, never as clutter.

Framing

Use thin black or soft-line borders around photos and cards. Corners can be softly rounded, but keep the overall feel crisp.

Captions

Captions should be small, uppercase, and practical: product name, location cue, or service note. Avoid poetic captions.

07 Photography

Real café light, real food, real space.

The provided images point to a clean, warm interior with sage walls, birch wood, black tables, greenery, coffee, pastries, salad and desserts. Keep that honesty. Avoid glossy stock-style café shots.

Environment: show the bright interior, windows, plants and seating so Lugo feels local and visitable.

Product groupings: coffee, pastry and cake on black tables for contrast.

Desserts: simple plating, enough negative space, natural shadows.

Lunch: fresh color, close detail, clean white tableware.

Service: counter, coffee machine and menu board should feel orderly and welcoming.

Use

Daylight, warm practical lighting, clean surfaces, real portions, hands in frame when natural, and wide shots that show where people sit.

Crop

Crop with confidence: full-bleed interiors for atmosphere, tight product crops for social posts, square crops for menu highlights.

Avoid

Over-saturated filters, fake steam, cluttered tables, extreme close-ups of foam, generic smiling stock models and heavy beige overlays.

09 Voice & copy

Plain, warm and useful.

Voice attributes

Local, calm, clear, welcoming and specific. Write like a café owner who knows the regulars and respects their time.

Sentence style

Short sentences. Concrete details. Put useful information first: what is served, when, where and how to order.

Use phrases like

  • Good coffee, fresh bakery, easy seats.
  • Pop in before the market.
  • Ask what’s fresh at the counter.
  • Hot or iced, to stay or take away.

Avoid phrases like

  • Experience the difference.
  • Crafted with passion.
  • Premium coffee journey.
  • Where quality meets comfort.

11 Do / Don’t

Keep it warm, but keep it disciplined.

Do

  • Use the logo in white on sage, black or calm pale fields.
  • Let white space and clear type do most of the work.
  • Use real interior, coffee, bakery and lunch photography.
  • Keep menu layouts aligned, separated by fine rules.
  • Write with plain local specificity.
  • Use sage as the main brand color, not every color at once.

Don’t

  • Place the seal over busy food photos without a clear panel.
  • Use cream-on-cream layouts that lose contrast.
  • Add generic café icons, random blobs or ornamental patterns.
  • Make the brand feel like a luxury hotel or chain coffee shop.
  • Use vague slogans or over-polished marketing language.
  • Over-filter food shots until they stop looking real.
12 Quick reference

The Lugo cheat sheet.

Core colors

Warm White #FFFDF8
Table Black #171514
Lugo Sage #7F9688
Birch Wood #EFDCB8
Matcha Green #A8B85E

Type hierarchy

Bold compact sans-serif headings. Clean system sans body. Uppercase micro-labels for opening times, location and menu sections.

Spacing rule

Use generous margins, 80–100px section spacing, 24px card gaps and thin rules to separate practical information.

Button style

Rounded pill buttons, uppercase labels, black primary, outline secondary, sage for order or visit CTAs.

Image style

Real café light, black table contrast, sage and birch interiors, honest food, calm crops, no heavy filters.

Most important rule

Make every touchpoint feel like a clear local invitation: easy to read, easy to visit, and recognisably Lugo.
