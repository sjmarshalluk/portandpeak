01 / About

A small café built around proper coffee, fresh bakes, and the people who come for them.

Specific beats over sentimental paragraphs. Lugo is a counter-service café — friendly, considered, and built for both ten-minute stops and slow Sunday mornings.

We opened Lugo to make the coffee shop we always wanted to find.

Unhurried, considered, and full of regulars who feel like they belong. Every cup is poured from beans roasted by independents we trust, and every bake leaves the kitchen the morning it's made.

The room follows the same logic. Pale timber, sage upholstery, black surfaces, white ceramics, plants on the sill, and enough quiet to read a book if you want to.
