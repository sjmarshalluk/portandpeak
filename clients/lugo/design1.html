Lugo, who?

A small place built around good coffee and the people who drink it.

We opened Lugo to make the kind of coffee shop we always wanted to find — unhurried, considered, and full of regulars who feel like they belong.

Every cup is poured from beans roasted by independents we trust. Our food is made in-house each morning using produce from local growers and bakers, and the menu shifts gently through the seasons.

The coffee

Single origins, dialled in daily.

A rotating house espresso plus filters from roasters across the UK and Europe.

We taste, weigh and time every brew, but we keep things friendly. If you're not sure what you're after, ask — we'll find the cup that fits.

On the menu

Made each morning.

Coffee

House espresso, filter, and a small specials list for those after something different.

Bakes

Pastries, cakes and pies baked fresh in our kitchen. Seasonal, and never the same week twice.

Brunch

A short, careful menu of plates that change with the seasons. Veg-forward, properly cooked.

The room

Somewhere to sit a little longer.

Warm light, quiet music and a counter you can lean on.

Whether you're stopping in for ten minutes between meetings or settling in for the morning with a book, the space is built for both.

"Creating a small, quiet community around really good coffee — that's what we're here for."
— The Lugo team
